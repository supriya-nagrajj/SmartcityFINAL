Travel Tips for Mumbai

Best Time to Visit

The ideal time to visit Mumbai is from October to March when the weather is pleasant.

Local Transport

Use local trains, buses, and auto-rickshaws for economical travel around the city.

Street Food

Don’t miss the iconic Vada Pav, Pav Bhaji, and Bhel Puri from Mumbai’s street vendors.
